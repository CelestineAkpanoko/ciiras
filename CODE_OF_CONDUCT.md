# Code of Conduct

CIIRAS follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

---

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the CIIRAS community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

We value:
- respectful technical disagreement
- thoughtful critique
- collaborative problem solving

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Welcoming newcomers
- Focusing on technical merit
- Acknowledging different perspectives

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or bad-faith arguments
- Publishing private information without consent

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.
They have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate it.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces:
- GitHub repositories
- Issues and pull requests
- Discussions and community forums

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
- Contact the maintainers privately
- Or email: **celestineakpanoko@gmail.com**  

All reports will be handled discreetly and respectfully.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Responses to violations may include:
- A warning
- Temporary or permanent ban
- Removal of content

The goal is to protect the community, not to punish.
